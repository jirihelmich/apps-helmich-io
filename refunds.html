Refund Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

App Store Purchases

Nighty is distributed exclusively through the Apple App Store. All purchases are processed by Apple, and all refunds are subject to Apple's refund policy.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, you must contact Apple directly:

  • Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.
  • Find Nighty in your purchase history and select "Request a Refund".

We are not able to process refunds directly, as Apple is the seller of record for all App Store purchases.

Contact

For questions about the app itself, contact nighty@helmich.io. For billing and refund inquiries, please contact Apple Support.
